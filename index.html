Our desserts are unique, we serve bites with a side of bytes. Baking is a science, so we've applied all things tech and science-y to our treats!

Cell Cookie
The mitochondria is the powerhouse of the cell and this cookie decorated like a cell will be the powerhouse of your afternoon.

Cinnamon Bunsen Stroopwafel
Bunsen burners are hot, hot, hot. These rolls are warm, warm, warm and full of cinnamony and iced goodness.

Quantum Cake
Nothing will get you moving like a quantum cupcake, iced with buttercream and featuring an atom shaped decoration on top.

Chemistry Canes

The Card Art Company was hosting an arts crafts night for a local Women in Tech coding group. They didn't want to spare any detail so they ordered our Megabyte tray. Each attendee was able to enjoy a host of different desserts as well as a fun science fact about each one. Good times were had by all.
