Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship?

Yes! We do ship. We can ship within the United States, internationally, we're even willing to try to ship to outer space, just in case there's a martian or two up there that might want to see what cake is all about.

What sizes are your dessert packages?

We have several! While we'd love to have binary multipliers, our packages are a little smaller. Our Kilo tray has 20 treats, the Megabyte tray has 40 treats, the Gigabyte tray has 60 treats and the Terabyte tray has 80 treats. Want more than that? We do custom sizing too!

What if I want a special science item for my event?

That's great! We'd love to nerd out with you. Just let us know what kind of design you have in mind, be it decorative or in a certain shape and we'll see what we can do. We want your event to be out of this world!

What if I want an art-themed dessert?

That's all right. We might not be the bakery for you, but we have a sister bakery called "Palettes and Pastries" that we can refer you out to. They'll whip up something colorful and museum-worthy for you in no time.
